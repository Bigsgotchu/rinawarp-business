What is a "soft launch"? +

A soft launch means we're making Terminal Pro available to a small group of early users before the official public launch. This gives us a chance to gather feedback, fix any issues, and make improvements based on real-world usage.

Is this the final version? +

No, this is an early access version. While all core features work, there may be some rough edges. Your feedback will help us polish and improve Terminal Pro before the official launch.

What if I find a bug? +

Please report bugs to karina@rinawarptech.com or through our feedback form. Bug reports during the soft launch help us immensely and may qualify you for special rewards!

How do I upgrade from free to Pro? +

You can upgrade at any time from within the terminal or by visiting our upgrade page. Your license will unlock all Pro features instantly.

Is my data safe? +

Absolutely. We take privacy seriously. Your terminal data stays on your machine, and we only collect anonymous usage statistics to improve the product.
