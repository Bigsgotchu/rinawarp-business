## 2. What Are Cookies?

### 2.1 Cookie Definition
Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website or use certain software. They help websites remember information about your visit and make your next visit easier and more useful to you.

### 2.2 Types of Cookies
We use different types of cookies:

- **Session Cookies**: Temporary cookies that expire when you close your browser
- **Persistent Cookies**: Remain on your device until they expire or you delete them
- **First-Party Cookies**: Set by our domains
- **Third-Party Cookies**: Set by our partners or service providers
- **Secure Cookies**: Transmitted only over HTTPS connections

### 2.3 Similar Technologies
We also use technologies similar to cookies:

- **Web Beacons**: Small graphic images used for tracking
- **Local Storage**: Browser-based data storage (LocalStorage, SessionStorage)
- **Pixel Tags**: Tiny images used for tracking
- **ETags**: Browser cache identifiers
- **Device Fingerprinting**: Identification based on device characteristics
