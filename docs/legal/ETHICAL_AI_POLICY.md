## 3. AI Development Guidelines

### 3.1 Responsible AI Development

- **Ethics review boards** for major AI projects
- **Impact assessments** before deployment
- **Bias testing** throughout development
- **Safety testing** for all AI systems
- **Red teaming** for critical applications

### 3.2 Data Ethics

- **Consent-based** data collection
- **Diverse and representative** training datasets
- **Bias detection** and mitigation
- **Data provenance** tracking
- **Synthetic data** where appropriate

### 3.3 Algorithm Design

- **Fairness-aware** algorithms
- **Interpretability** by design
- **Robustness** to adversarial attacks
- **Fail-safe** mechanisms
- **Human-in-the-loop** for critical decisions

## 5. Prohibited AI Applications

### 5.1 Unethical Use Cases

- **Weapons development** or autonomous weapons
- **Mass surveillance** or oppression tools
- **Deceptive practices** (deepfakes for fraud)
- **Exploitative applications**
- **Harmful content** generation

### 5.2 High-Risk Applications

- **Medical diagnosis** without human oversight
- **Legal decision-making** without review
- **Autonomous vehicles** without proper safeguards
- **Financial trading** without risk controls
- **Critical infrastructure** without fail-safes
