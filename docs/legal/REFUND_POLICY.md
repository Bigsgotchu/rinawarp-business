## 3. Refunds We Do Provide (Strict Conditions)

Refunds are issued **only** if:

### 3.1 The Service Failed to Deliver (Technical Failure)

You may qualify for a refund if:

- Terminal Pro license activation failed and our support team cannot resolve the issue
- AI Music Video Creator credits were consumed but no output was generated due to a system error
- Stripe processed a payment but no product access was delivered

We require:

- Proof of purchase (Stripe receipt)
- Reasonable attempt to resolve the issue with support
- Error logs or screenshots (if applicable)

### 3.2 Duplicate or Unauthorized Charges

If Stripe incorrectly charges you:

- Duplicate payments
- Accidental subscription renewals
- Invalid or unauthorized transactions

We will refund the mistaken charge after verification.

### 3.3 Subscription Refunds (Pro-Rated Only If Required)

If you cancel a subscription:

- Your subscription remains active until the end of the billing period
- No refunds are provided for partial months
- If a billing-cycle failure occurs (e.g., Stripe charges early or incorrectly), we will correct it.
