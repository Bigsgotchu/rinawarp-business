Refund Policy

RinaWarp Tech - Customer Satisfaction Guarantee

30-Day Money-Back Guarantee

We're confident you'll love RinaWarp products. That's why we offer a full 30-day money-back guarantee on all purchases.

If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, you can request a full refund within 30 days of your original purchase date.

Refund Eligibility

To be eligible for a refund:

  • Request must be made within 30 days of original purchase
  • Product must be in original condition (for physical products)
  • Digital products: No excessive usage or redistribution
  • Valid proof of purchase required

Refund Process

  1. Contact Us: Email us with your refund request
  2. Provide Details: Include your order number and reason for refund
  3. Review: We'll process your request within 2-3 business days
  4. Refund: Approved refunds will be processed within 5-7 business days

Non-Refundable Items

The following items are not eligible for refunds:

  • Products purchased more than 30 days ago
  • Custom or personalized orders (unless defective)
  • Downloaded digital products that have been extensively used
  • Products damaged by misuse or normal wear

Partial Refunds

In certain situations, partial refunds may be granted:

  • Digital products with significant usage beyond trial period
  • Physical products with minor damage (restocking fee may apply)
  • Software licenses with long-term usage

Digital Product Specifics

Downloaded Software & Apps: Refunds available if the product doesn't function as described or has critical defects.

For digital products, we may require:

  • Description of the issue encountered
  • System information and error logs (if applicable)
  • Confirmation that the product was not redistributed
