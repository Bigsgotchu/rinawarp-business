Not yet. A permanent Free tier is on the roadmap, but right now the active offer is the Founder Wave lifetime license. The pricing page will be updated as soon as Free and standard subscriptions go live.

What exactly do I get with Founder Wave?

You get lifetime access to RinaWarp Terminal Pro™ as it evolves: dev scripts, deployment helpers, security checks, and infrastructure improvements within the Terminal Pro product scope.

Are refunds available?

There is no generic "one click money-back" promise on this page. If something goes seriously wrong with your purchase, you can reach out through Support and we will do our best to make it right.

No. The Founder Wave license is for RinaWarp Terminal Pro™. Other apps like the AI Music Video Creator™ may have their own pricing and licensing.

Is this production-ready today?

RinaWarp Terminal Pro is actively evolving. The goal is practical, inspectable scripts and infrastructure helpers – not magic. You should still review configs, backups, and security rules like you would with any serious stack.
