Refund Policy

This page describes the general refund approach for RinaWarp products, including RinaWarp Terminal Pro. If you have a specific question about your purchase, please contact support.

Digital Software Purchases

Because RinaWarp Terminal Pro is delivered as digital software with license keys, we offer limited refunds under specific conditions, such as:

  • Technical issues that cannot be resolved after reasonable troubleshooting with our support team.
  • Accidental duplicate purchases of the same product/license.

Refund Window

Where applicable, refund requests generally must be submitted within a limited time after purchase (for example, 14–30 days, depending on the offer or promotion at the time of purchase). Any specific refund window will be clearly stated on the corresponding sales page or offer.

How to Request a Refund

  • Contact us via the Support page.
  • Include your purchase email, order ID, and a brief description of the issue.
  • We may request additional information or troubleshooting steps to try to resolve the problem.

Non-Refundable Situations

We may decline refunds in cases such as:

  • Change of mind after extensive use of the product.
  • License sharing, abuse, or violation of the terms of use.
  • Refund requests made well outside the stated refund window for a particular offer.

This policy is a general guideline and may be updated or adjusted over time. Certain promotions or plans may have their own specific refund terms, which will control if different from the general policy on this page.
