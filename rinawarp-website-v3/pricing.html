Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change plans anytime?

Yes! You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your plan at any time. Changes take effect at the next billing cycle, and we'll prorate any charges.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express) and PayPal. Enterprise customers can also pay via invoice with net 30 terms.

Is there a free trial?

Yes! All plans come with a 14-day free trial. No credit card required for Starter, but a card is needed for Professional and Enterprise trials.

What happens to my data if I cancel?

You retain access to your account and all your data for 30 days after cancellation. After that, your data is securely deleted according to our privacy policy.

Do you offer refunds?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all paid plans. If you're not satisfied, contact support for a full refund, no questions asked.
