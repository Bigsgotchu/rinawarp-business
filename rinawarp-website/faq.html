Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions about RinaWarp Terminal Pro and the AI Music Video Creator.

Licensing & Pricing

What's the difference between Lifetime and Monthly plans?

Monthly plans give you access as long as your subscription is active. Lifetime plans are a one-time payment that grants ongoing access for the lifetime of the product, including updates covered by your license terms.

How many devices can I install Terminal Pro on?

You may install Terminal Pro on multiple devices you personally own, as long as usage stays within the terms of your license (no sharing or reselling). For team usage, contact us about an appropriate plan.

Do you offer refunds?

Yes, we offer a limited refund window for qualifying purchases. Please see the Refund Policy for full details and eligibility.

Installation & Activation

I didn't receive my license email. What should I do?

First, check your Spam/Promotions folders for an email with the subject "RinaWarp Terminal Pro License." If you don't see it within 10–15 minutes, contact support with your purchase email, and we'll locate or resend it.

How do I activate my license?

Install and launch Terminal Pro, then open the license window from the main menu and paste your license key. You can follow the full guide on the Activation Instructions page.
