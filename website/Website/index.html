Yes. The backend is live on Oracle with PM2, Caddy, Cloudflare, SSL, Stripe, license management, health checks and security hardening already in place. You're not buying a prototype – you're plugging into a working system.

What do I get with a lifetime license?

Lifetime updates to Terminal Pro, full access to the secured backend, license-based activation for the desktop app, and the right to use it in your own workflows and client projects. Founder & Pioneer tiers are strictly limited for scarcity and early-believer flex.

∞

Is this only for devs?

No. It's dev-grade, but built for creators, power users, and anyone who lives in their computer. If you're managing projects, content, releases, or multiple tools, Terminal Pro helps you automate and stay in control.
