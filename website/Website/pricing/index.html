Pricing FAQ

Common questions about plans and licensing.

What's the difference between monthly and lifetime?

Monthly plans give you flexibility – pay as you go, cancel anytime. Lifetime plans are one-time payments that give you permanent access to Terminal Pro, all future updates, and new features. Founder & Pioneer tiers are limited and won't be available forever.

Can I upgrade from monthly to lifetime?

Yes! You can upgrade to a lifetime tier at any time. Your monthly payments won't be credited, but you'll lock in lifetime access and stop paying monthly fees.

What happens when Founder tier sells out?

Once all 200 Founder licenses are sold, that tier closes forever. Pioneer tier ($800) opens next with 300 licenses. After that, only the evergreen Lifetime Future tier ($999) remains available.

Do lifetime licenses include commercial use?

Yes. All lifetime tiers include full commercial use rights. Use Terminal Pro for client work, sell services built with it, or integrate it into your business – no restrictions.

Is there a refund policy?

Monthly plans can be cancelled anytime with no refund for partial months. Lifetime purchases have a 14-day money-back guarantee – if Terminal Pro isn't for you, we'll refund your purchase within 14 days.

Can I use one license on multiple machines?

Yes. Your license key works on all your personal devices. For team/commercial use with multiple users, contact us for volume licensing.
